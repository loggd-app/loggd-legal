Delete Your Data – Loggd

Loggd stores all your data locally on your device. No data is stored on our servers. You have full control over your data at all times.

Delete all data in the app

1. Open Loggd
2. Go to Settings (gear icon, top right)
3. Scroll to Data section
4. Tap Delete All Data
5. Confirm the deletion

This permanently deletes all categories, entries, photos, reminders, and settings.

Delete data by uninstalling

Uninstalling Loggd from your device removes all app data, as everything is stored locally.

Third-party data

If you used the feedback form, your message was sent via Formspree. Crash reports are collected by Sentry, and subscription data is managed by RevenueCat. To request deletion of this data, contact us below.
